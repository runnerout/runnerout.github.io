The Beach

The Beach is a 2000 film directed by Danny Boyle based on the 1996 book of the same name written by Alex Garland. It stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Richard, a traveller seeking new and perhaps dangerous experiences in Thailand, where he receives a map that leads him to a mythical island.

The film was initially poorly received, having a rating of 4/10, however over time that rose to 6.6/10. The biggest problem people have with the film are the characters, with whom nobody can seem to sympathize. Personally, i like this film not for the characters or the story, but rather for artistic reasons. The director did a superb job, the colors of the film evoke a feeling of warmth, and throughout the film there is a feeling of mystery.

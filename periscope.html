Down Periscope

Down Periscope is a 1996 film directed by David S. Ward. It stars Kelsey Grammer as a newly promoted submarine captain going on his first mission to play the opposing force in a wargame with a boat and crew that most would consider to be subpar.

I like this movie because it is genuinely funny, the timing of the jokes is great, the actors obviously knew what they were doing.
